Privacy Policy

We value the privacy of our customers. We are always careful to secure the users' information. This information will be used only by authorized employees. Our employees will not solicit OTP or Passcode from customers for any reason. They will be notified by telephone when necessary.

Users can log in with a passcode or fingerprint. They do not need to login again until they log out. However, they will need to retype the passcode every time they make a transfer or bill, and they will not be able to use one account on both phones at the same time.

We constantly review our systems and information to provide the best possible service to our customers.

Gathering information from users

We collect only the necessary information for the Dongfang Pay Application and to understand the financial needs of the users.

  • Gender
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Father’s name
  • Financial information
  • Pay records
  • Photographs
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies
  • Device model
  • Last login success date
  • First activated date
  • Last reactivated date
  • Last Invalid Login Attempt
  • Last Invalid Login Attempt Channel

The above information is stored to protect the security of our users as a matter of priority. This information will not be returned to any organization. To resolve potential disputes and issues and to be reserved for the full range of our services, it is stored.
